Introduction

This is the group project for group 7. It was made by Sebastian Murden, Jack Gillam, and Joshua Rowe. In it we investigate recurrence relations.

What is a Recurrence Relation?

The website Math Insight defines a recurrence relation as "an equation that defines a sequence based on a rule that gives the next term as a function of the previous term(s)." The relations we have investigated in this project are the Mandelbrot set, the Fibonacci sequence, and the real life occurences of the Golden Ratio.
